# THE DISSOLUTION OF THERA-VAIN
## Episode 1 — "What It Felt Like to Stop Being"
### Directed by hand. Written by The Witness.

**Logline:** A being of pure light approaches an impossible threshold — and meets her opposite. What follows is not war. It is unmaking. And then, a choice that changes everything.

**Runtime:** ~75 seconds
**Universe:** THE TEMPORAL BRIDGES
**Mood Arc:** Reverence → Wonder → Violence → Stillness → Awe → Grief
**Color Grade:** Cool aurora teal + warm observation gold. As Thera-Vain is unmade, the gold drains from the image.
**Music:** Low drone building. Crystalline chimes on the beauty shots. Silence at the pause. Swelling strings on the sacrifice. Single sustained note on the final line.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Total shots:** 8

**Keyframe model:** Runway Gen-4 Image Turbo (or Nano Banana for higher quality stills)
**Video model:** Veo 3.1 (image-to-video mode for consistency)
**Voice:** Gemini TTS — Kore voice, slow, measured, haunted
**Music:** Generate ambient score with building strings via Suno or Pollinations

**Post-production (manual or future automation):**
- Speed ramp on Shot 3 (smash cut → slow motion shatter)
- Color grade: warm gold → cool teal shift as Thera-Vain dissolves
- Silence timing on Shot 5 is CRITICAL — don't rush it
- Final fade to black should be SLOW (3 full seconds)

**Character consistency:**
- Generate Thera-Vain keyframe in Shot 1 first
- Use that image as reference for ALL subsequent shots with her
- Kess-Void appears in Shot 4 — generate separately then composite
- Shot 5-7 need both characters — use Veo Ingredients feature with both references
